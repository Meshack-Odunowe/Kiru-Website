Our Mission

To power a new, prosperous and self-sustaining Africa.

Kiru is a “clean-tech” company providing smart clean energy solutions for businesses and households in Africa. Our one mission is to power progress in Africa by making clean energy solutions readily accessible for businesses and households.

Our Vision

To be a leading multinational clean energy powerhouse

We are building a future where everyone living and working in Africa
can have the infrastructure and the environment to make their own
dreams come true.

September 2019

The Birth

Kiru is coined from the African phrase ‘Nke Iru Ka’ used by the Igbo people to inspire hope, faith,
resilience and courage. The phrase means, ‘the future holds greater things’. At Kiru, we believe that
the future is possible and limitless. On September 30, 2019, Kiru was founded and incorporated
in Nigeria to set the stage for the future!
